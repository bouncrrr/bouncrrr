troubleshooting

issue possible solution
bouncrrr immediately shows an error after switching over to logic when I press 'bounce stems'. turn off "check for active click" and "check for active solo" in bounce settings.
logic has stopped midway through the bounce process. use the "choose track number to start from" function within bouncrrr to resume from where it stopped.
bouncrrr fails to get track names, or switch to logic at all. make sure your permissions are set up correctly. try removing bouncrrr's accessibility permission in system preferences and restarting bouncrrr to set it up again.
logic keeps on crashing during the bounce process. bouncrrr simply automates the bounce process, it cannot prevent crashes in logic. make sure all of your plugins are stable and maybe try a different version of logic pro if the issues persist.
my bounces are including sends from other tracks. this can happen due to the way logic handles soloing and sends. try to avoid pre-fader sends and also mute all tracks before starting bouncrrr, making sure you don't mute any folders or groups that are necessary to hear stems you want to bounce.

for anything else, please email us at support@bouncrrr.co
